A US-based law firm was hit by a Google update, losing significant traffic. I audited their 10,000+ page site, identified 97% of it as thin or 'zombie' content, and executed a strategy to prune it down to ~300 high-value URLs. This restored crawl efficiency and stopped the bleeding.
